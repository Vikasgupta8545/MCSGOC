About us


Dream, Desire And Develop At MCSGOC


Dr. M. C. Saxena Group of Colleges (MCSGOC), Lucknow, is a foundation of belief and virtuousness. It is the manifestation of the aspiration of our Founder, Professor (Dr.) M. C. SAXENA. MCSGOC envisages an education system that is based on vision and innovation that ignites the quiescent talents of its students. With our motto of ‘Padate hai Hum’ we continue to educate our students for the dynamic wold. Innovative and scientific approach in academics, along with MCSGOC extra curricular activities with a blend of values from the city of Nawabs help in making its students a responsible citizen.
